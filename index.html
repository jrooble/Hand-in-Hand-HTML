Hello, this is a website about Minecraft.

Minecraft is a game that consists of either creating big machines and builds or trying to survive with monsters trying to kill you. In survival mode, you will have to get enough materials and weapons to be able to survive. In creative mode, you can build anything without having to find the resources you need to build them.

There are many tiers of weapons, each with a different durability and attack damage. Here are some tables.
